I'm V Méshall Brown.

I didn't start writing to have answers. I started because some things wouldn't leave me alone.

What started this wasn't written in ink— but by the Spirit.

I've learned to pay attention to what stays— what lingers, what presses, what asks something of me.

This space is where I write from that place. Not after I've figured it out. While I'm still in it.

You won't find quick conclusions here. But you will find honesty.
